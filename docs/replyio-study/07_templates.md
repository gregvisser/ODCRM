# Reply.io Study - Templates

## Exploration Date
2026-01-24

## Current Status
**TEMPLATES** - ✅ Email templates library observed.

## Templates Page (Observed)

### Header Controls
- **All sequences** dropdown filter.
- **New template** button.
- **Search** input.

### Sections
- **Personal** section.
- **Workspace** section.
- **Library** section with categories:
  - General
  - Sales
  - Recruiting
  - Business Development
  - Inbound Sales
  - Follow‑up
  - Outbound Sales
  - PR & Link‑building

### Template Cards
Each card displays:
- **Template name**
- **Preview snippet** (short excerpt of email body)
- **Open rate** and **reply rate** metrics
- **Merge fields** are present in snippets (e.g., personalization tokens)

## Assumptions (Explicit)
- The library view appears to include community templates and personal/workspace templates.
- Selecting a template likely opens a preview/editor view, but editing fields were not expanded in this session.
